Honest answers, while you're asking.

Will AI really get my class right?

The model sees your full class profile, prior feedback, and the actual NCCA strands and strand units for the grade band you teach. It names real strands, not invented ones. And you can rate every lesson — the next draft learns from it. When it misses, it's one click to regenerate with a different pitch.

Is my data safe?

EU-region hosting. Row-level security means nobody else sees your data, full stop. No pupil information is ever stored — Planner Bee is teacher-facing only. You can export or delete everything any time.

How much does it cost?

Free while in beta. When paid plans land, we'll give beta teachers a fair deal and plenty of notice. We're not here to nickel-and-dime a profession that's already stretched.

What if I just want to write it myself?

You can. Every lesson can be overridden with your own content, your own materials, your own flow. Planner Bee is a starting point, never a straitjacket — take as much or as little as you like.

What if the 2023 curriculum changes?

We update the reference. You don't do anything. Your plans keep working.

I'm not great with tech.

One button to generate. One button to print. That's most of it. Enter your email, we send you a link, you click it — no password to remember.
